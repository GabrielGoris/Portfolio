3D hobby

I started my hobby as work. As time went on and the search for a job became increasingly complex, an idea came to me. I invested in a 3D printer and set up a shop using models found on the internet. However, after some time, I began studying the subject and started using the software Blender to bring my ideas to life.

Now, it's just a hobby that I like to spend my time on when I'm not studying programming or working on projects.

Writing

Since I was a child, I have always been passionate about books. I would always want to visit second-hand bookstores, bookshops, and look for new books. This passion continued as I grew up. Along with this passion, a dream grew with me from a young age: to have my name in a book.

I achieved this thanks to a publisher called Elixir during an "Escritubro," an idea inspired by "Inktober," which takes place in October with a series of 31 drawings, one for each day of the month. In this case, it was 31 micro-stories, created with themes chosen by the publisher via Instagram. In the end, some of the stories were selected, and I was honored to be chosen to participate in the book!
